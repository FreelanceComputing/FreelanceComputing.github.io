Stranger things at the Jemaa el-Fnaa Square night market

. . .

“Hello!

Hello, beautiful lady.

Come please.

Yes, beautiful lady.

You like henna?

Henna is good.

Is good for no bad luck.

Is good for sexy, sexy.

See?

See?

See my own hand.

I show you.

No problem.

It no take time.

Only 3 minutes.

Is very, very nice.

I show you what I do.

You no pay.

I show you.

Is free.

You see?

Is very nice on your hand.

You see?

Very nice flower.

You like it?

Very nice, eh?

Is 300 dirham.

Yes, 300.

I want my money.

Please give my money.

I no take 100 dirham.

I no take 200 dirham.

Is 300 dirham.”
