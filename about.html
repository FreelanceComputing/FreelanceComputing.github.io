About

Areas Covered

Here are the main areas that I cover: Stone, Trentham, Barlaston, Moddershall, Oulton, Meaford, Tittensor, Aston-by-Stone, Yarnfield, Hilderstone, Spot Acre and Stafford North.

If your area isn't listed above, you can still go ahead and contact me as I may be able to remotely connect to your computer in order to solve your problem.

The Process Explained

You can contact me via the phone number and email address provided on the contact page of this website. I will always endeavour to respond to all requests within 24 hours. When I get back to you I'll ask you to explain your problem in more detail if required. This will enable me to judge how much time the job will take. At this point we can both agree on a date and time.

Pricing

Callout jobs and remote support are charged slightly differently.

Callout Jobs

Minimum charge for each callout job is £30 for the first hour and then £25 per hour thereafter.

Remote Support

Minimum charge for remote connection support is £25 for the first hour and then £25 per hour thereafter.
