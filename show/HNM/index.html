Hidden No More Cameroon is a not-for-profit organisation which celebrates the achievements of Cameroonian STEM women and girls, carries out activities to encourage primary school girls to take on STEM courses and creates a pool of role models and a network of mentors and promote STEM girl mentoring in Colleges and Universities.
