E-commerce for Tiger of Sweden

Tiger of Sweden reached out to us wanting a total revamp of their Salesforce E-commerce platform. With Tiger of Sweden’s 115 year long history of making premium garments, they were undergoing a full rebrand and wanted their new E-commerce to reflect their heritage as well as have all the qualities of an up-to-date online shopping experience with the customer in focus.

The end result is an easy to use, mobile first, customer-centric shopping experience that fits right in with the latest standars within digital shopping. Something that inspires the customer without hindering the shopping experience.

Visit the site

During 6 months we initiated workshops and focus-groups, laying the foundation of what our final UX-approach was to be. After nailing down the customer journey, together with having all stakeholders on the same page, we started our design process – working itterative with weekly meetings together with the developers & E-com team at Tiger of Sweden.
