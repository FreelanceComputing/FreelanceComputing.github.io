Giving voice to depression

. . .

I’m tired of this life

I’m tired of hoping

I’m tired of praying

I’m tired of holding it in

I’m tired of my struggles

I’m tired of feeling trapped

I’m tired of feeling helpless

I’m tired of saying "I'm okay"

I’m tired of explaining myself

I’m tired of my responsibilities

I’m tired of not wanting to talk

I’m tired of being misunderstood

I’m tired of not being able to talk

I’m tired of putting on a brave face

I’m tired of barely keeping myself afloat

I’m tired of not being the best version of myself

I’m tired of trying to be the best version of myself

I’m tired of believing things will eventually get better

I’m tired of being tired
